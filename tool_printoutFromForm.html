    How to Use This Tool
    
                            
    Welcome to the Printout From Form Tool! This tool lets you generate customizable printouts from your Form.io forms. Here’s how to get started:
    
                            
      
                                
    1. Upload Your Form: Navigate to the Converter tab and select your Form.io JSON file to load your form into the tool.
      2. 
                                
    2. Customize Your Printout: Navigate to the Settings tab and adjust the layout options to your preference. Finalize the changes by pressing "Apply Settings". Your settings will save for future printouts.
      3. 
                                
    3. Download Your Printout: Scroll down to the DevExpress JSON section to download your printout, or copy the JSON to your clipboard.
      4. 
                                
    4. Import Your Printout: Navigate to the DevExpress Reports page and press the "Import" button at the top of the page.
      5. 
                                
    5. Upload Your Printout: Select your DevExpress report JSON file, and verify that the report Name and Department have generated as expected.
      6. 
                                
    6. Verify Layout: After importing your printout, review the report layout to ensure that everything has generated as expected.
      7. 
                                
    7. Retrieve SQL: Navigate back to the Printout Tool, and scroll down to the SQL Query section, and copy/paste the generated SQL queries into SSMS.
      8. 
                                
    8. Verify SQL: Carefully review the SQL that was generated, and ensure that it meets your printout's needs. Once you have done so you can execute the SP Create/Alter statements.
      9. 
                                
    9. Add Your DataSource: Add your Stored Procedures into your printout's DataSource like normal.
      10. 
                                
    10. Test Your Printout: Finally, run your printout to ensure that everything is working as expected.
      11.

    Known Issues
    
                            
      
                                
    • Nested Grids are not supported. Grids nested within other components will not render correctly.
      • 
                                
    • Nested Form components are not supported. These will be rendered as placeholder labels.
      • 
                                
    • Container component vertical scaling is overly generous, which may lead to excessive spacing in the printout.
      • 
                                
    • For more information on supported functionality, please review the Patch Notes..
      • 
                                
    • If you encounter an undocumented issue, please reach out to me with details.
