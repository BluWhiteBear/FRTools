Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how UniTools ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our website and tools (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information.

UniTools is a serverless, client-side application. All tools operate entirely within your web browser. Any data you input, paste, or upload into the Service is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers, as we do not operate any servers or databases.

When you close or refresh the browser page, all data you have entered is permanently discarded.

2. Local Storage

We do not use cookies. However, certain tools within the Service use your browser's local storage to save your preferences (such as layout settings and font choices) so they persist between sessions. This data:

  • Is stored entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Contains only tool configuration preferences, not personal information
  • Can be cleared at any time through your browser settings

We do not use local storage or any other technologies for tracking or analytics purposes.

3. Third-Party Hosting

The Service is hosted on GitHub Pages. While we do not collect any data, GitHub, as our hosting provider, may automatically collect certain technical information as part of their standard operations, which may include:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time of access

This information is collected and processed by GitHub in accordance with the GitHub Privacy Statement. We do not have access to this data.

4. Third-Party Resources

The Service loads external resources from third-party content delivery networks (CDNs) to provide functionality and styling. These providers may collect standard web server logs when your browser requests these resources:

  • jsDelivr (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — Bootstrap framework
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — Web fonts
  • Cloudflare (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — Prism.js syntax highlighting

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers for information about their data practices.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, this policy applies equally to all users regardless of age.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect, transmit, or store any user data, there is no user data at risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, or breach on our part. All data you enter into the Service remains on your local device and under your control.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
